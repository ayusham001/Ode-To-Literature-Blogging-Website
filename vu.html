A call to VIRTUAL YOU!



Come sit in this twilight beside me, my darling.
Tell me those stories as you always do,
The stories of our fears and our fantasies,
And you of us, that has been due on you.

Sing me those lores while I watch the stars
Which reflect themselves utterly into my eyes and yours,
Talk to me of the love we share
And we'll watch, how then my heart cures,

Listening you peach 'bout the past, the future; The past of holding hands and assembling lips,
Of the struggles and the mess that became ours
And that will rest ours, until the apocalypse.

So, when you see our faces decipher into that dark
Hold hard my hand then, and lyric to me further,
The future of our shared dusks and dawns,
Of the love and the happiness dealt together.

Then, I'll narrate you the places we'll wander 'n' polaroid,
The verses and vocals that I'll sing you someday.
Now, just gaze at me, as you once before did
And let me feel your heat, all the while you stay.

Then on the closure, we'll stir our eyes to the east,
At last keeping the quiet to see
The sun come up, yet ever serene
And so like us, forever untamed and free.
